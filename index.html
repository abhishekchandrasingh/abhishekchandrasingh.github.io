The AI FinOps Paradigm: COGS to Revenue

An executive framework detailing why AI efficiency must drive product expansion rather than headcount reduction.

Strategy Model

Paradigm: The Cost-Cut Trap (AI vs. Headcount)

Treating AI solely as a tool to automate developer headcount turns technology into a zero-sum cost equation. As AI adoption scales, compute infrastructure expenses (API licenses, specialized vector indexes, GPU resource bounds) shift directly into COGS, offsetting marginal staff savings.

Critical Bottleneck: Flat business model with rising operational costs. Engineering becomes a pure cost center, locked in constant debates over compute budgets vs. staff reductions.
