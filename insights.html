Enterprise AI initiatives often stumble due to fragmented data pipelines, unclear success metrics, and missing Human‑in‑the‑Loop governance. A disciplined architecture that enforces data provenance, model validation, and cost‑aware deployment mitigates these risks.

Human‑in‑the‑Loop Architecture

Azure MCP Explained
